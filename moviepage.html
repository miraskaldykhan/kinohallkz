The animated film “The Lion King” is a remake of the 1994 animated film of the same name and tells about the adventures of a brave lion cub named Simba, who happened to be born and grow up on the expanses of the African savannah. The wise and strong lion of Mufas is the king of pride, and his son Simba is the direct heir


The brother of the lion king, Scar, dreams of taking his place and does everything possible for this. One day, Mufasa dies, and the baby Simba was expelled from the lands of the pride by the proclaimed King Scar. Ahead of the lion cub awaits many exciting and dangerous adventures, as well as a return to their native lands in order to return their rightful throne.
